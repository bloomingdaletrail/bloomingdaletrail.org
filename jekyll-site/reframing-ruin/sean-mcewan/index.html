For me, these images capture the beauty I see in the Bloomingdale Trail. Despite its current condition of neglect, the Trail offers an incredibly rich experience to those who walk along its path. From the scattered remnants of the past to the freshly painted graffiti, it is this layering of time and textures that I sought to document.
