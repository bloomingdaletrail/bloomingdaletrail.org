For those of you who use the social networking website Twitter, you can now follow Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail @thebloomingdale. We’ll continue to blog regularly but we’ll use Twitter to post quick updates.

If you aren’t familiar with Twitter, it lets you post short messages from your mobile phone via text message or via the Twitter website, and you can “follow” others who do the same and read their posts. It’s similar to blogging but meant for shorter, more timely updates.
