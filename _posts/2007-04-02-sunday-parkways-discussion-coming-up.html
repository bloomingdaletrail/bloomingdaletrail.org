--- permalink: /blog/2007/04/02/sunday-parkways-discussion-coming-up/ title: Sunday Parkways Discussion coming up author: Paul Smith layout: post published: true ---

Our friends at Chicagoland Bicycle Federation are hosting a discussion Wednesday, April 11, 2007, from 6:30 to 7:30 P.M. to go over the Sunday Parkways program. It’s a chance for you to learn about it, which would turn certain Chicago streets into parks for a few hours every Sunday, and to voice your concerns, comments, and questions.

Definitely check it out: head on over to Upcoming.org for details and you can let everyone know if you plan to attend. See you there!
