The Trust for Public Land-sponsored After School Matters program will be rolling out its product at a small party this Wednesday, August 5, between noon and 2pm, at 1800 N. Artesian.  An oral history and photo essay report, focusing on the neighborhoods along the Bloomingdale right of way, east of California Avenue, will be available to be picked up.  Meet the youth that produced this and have some food!
