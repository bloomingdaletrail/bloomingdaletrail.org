Save the Date- Saturday, October 10, 2009

9:30am - Noon

Before the winter brings its snow, give Albany-Whipple Park a chance to grow!

Come weed, plant bulbs, clean up, paint pumpkins, have fun and spend time with your neighbors and bring some gloves for digging!

Join The Trust for Public Land and The Friends of The Bloomingdale Trail at the 2nd Annual Service Day at Albany Whipple Park (1811 N Albany Ave)

Sign up to join the Albany-Whipple Park Committee and receive emails relating to the Park and Bloomingdale Trail events!

Delicious coffee provided by Starbucks, Donuts to munch on, and refreshing Apple Juice!
