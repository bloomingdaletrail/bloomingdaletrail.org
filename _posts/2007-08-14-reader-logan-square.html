This week’s Chicago Reader is a special issue on Logan Square, one in a series the Reader is doing about Chicago’s neighborhoods. The Bloomingdale Trail is featured as one of Logan Square’s highlights. Of course, it’s also a highlight of Humboldt Park, Wicker Park, and Bucktown, as well as the rest of the City, but we’re proud that people are starting to see the Trail as a prominent part of their neighborhood’s future.
