--- permalink: /blog/2009/07/22/caps-smoke-out-albany-whipple-park/ title: CAPS "Smoke-Out" at Albany-Whipple Park author: Ben Helphand layout: post published: true ---

CAPS 1421 will hold an outdoor meeting and 'Smoke-Out' (hot dogs, chips, and water sponsored by the 14th District CAPS office) this coming Weds. July 22nd at the new Albany-Whipple Park located at 1800 N. Whipple/1800 N. Albany. The 'Smoke Out' starts at 6:30 p.m. with a short CAPS meeting at 7 p.m.

This is a joint venture by CAPS 1421 and the Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail. Come join with neighbors and friends as we 'greet' the new park.

Hot dogs, chips, and water will be provided. But please feel free to bring dessert.
