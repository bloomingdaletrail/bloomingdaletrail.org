As many of you may know, the deadline recently passed for the submission of proposals to the city of Chicago for the preliminary design of the Bloomingdale Trail. Twenty-three teams have made submissions, which is a high level of interest (the City requires each team to be lead by a pre-qualified firm, of which there are just more than 50).

If you would like to see the list of the leaders for each team, visit the Chicago Department of Prourement Service’s Bid Tabulation database, and enter 69169 in the “Specification Number” field, and click “Search”.
