NOTE NEW TIME AND LOCATION

This summer the Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail helped fund a new community mural in Humboldt Park on the Bloomingdale embankment opposite the future access point at Albany Whipple Park. Join the Logan Square Neighborhood Association and Chicago Public Art Group in honoring the youth apprentices and residents who participated in this amazing mural!

WHEN: Thursday, Sept 9, 2010, 4:30pm-5:30pm

WHERE: Chicago Public Library-Humboldt Branch 1605 N. Troy (corner of North Ave)

Refreshments will be served following the program.

The mural lead by CPAG artist: Juan Carlos Perez, William Nicholson and Patricia Sotarello

Special thanks to our funders: The Field Foundation of Illinois, the Chicago Foundation for Women, Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail, The Trust for Public Land, Illinois Dept of Human Services, “Put Illinois to Work”

For more information call, Brenda Vega at CPAG, 312.427.2724 x11.
