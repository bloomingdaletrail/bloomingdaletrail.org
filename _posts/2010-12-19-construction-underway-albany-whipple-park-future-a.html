--- permalink: /blog/2010/12/19/construction-underway-albany-whipple-park-future-a/ title: Construction Underway on the Albany-Whipple Park (Future Access Point) author: Ben Helphand layout: post published: true ---

Despite snow & cold, construction at the Albany-Whipple Park proceeds! The park will be a future access point to the Bloomingdale Trail. The new park will featuring a climbing web, spider sculpture, and a long sitting wall decorated with nature themes, created by neighbors in a series of workshops led by the Chicago Public Art Group.
