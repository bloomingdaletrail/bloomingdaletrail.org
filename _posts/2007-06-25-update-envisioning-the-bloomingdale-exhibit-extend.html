--- permalink: /blog/2007/06/25/update-envisioning-the-bloomingdale-exhibit-extend/ title: Update: Envisioning the Bloomingdale exhibit extended author: Paul Smith layout: post published: true ---

Great news: the Envisioning the Bloomingdale exhibit has been extended through July 31st. The hours at Acme Art Works are Thursday, Friday, and Saturday, 12-5pm.

If you haven’t seen it yet, here’s your chance to catch a creative and visually stunning array of transformations of the Bloomingdale space. As the Trail inches closer to reality, the gap between our imaginations of it and the final product is also beginning to narrow. So come get a glimpse of what may be in our near future …
