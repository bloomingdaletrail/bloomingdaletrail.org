Last Spring CPS teachers and students from Yates and Moos Elementary Schools explored the Bloomingdale Trail using FBT's Education Resource Packets. Each had recieved a small educational grant from FBT to use the Trail to build a greater understanding of their neighbrohood. Classes produced posters, scrapbooks, postcards, and photographs.

Grants for 2010-2011 are available now. The $300 - $500 grant can be used for the completion of an activity in the Educational Resource Packet or for a project of your making. The grant Application will be reviewed on a rolling basis.
