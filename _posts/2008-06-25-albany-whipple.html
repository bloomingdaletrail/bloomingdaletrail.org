--- permalink: /blog/2008/06/25/albany-whipple/ title: Important public meeting this Thursday author: Paul Smith layout: post published: true ---

One of the Bloomingdale Trail access points will be a park on the lot directly north of the Trail, between Albany Ave. and Whipple St. The Chicago Park District is applying for a grant that will help develop the park. They are holding a meeting this Thursday, June 26th, at 6:30 p.m. at Simons Park, 1640 N. Drake Ave., to present their proposed plans for the grant, and to take feedback about them.

This is a public meeting, and it’s a great opportunity for you to come out and show your support for the Bloomingdale Trail.

See you Thursday!
