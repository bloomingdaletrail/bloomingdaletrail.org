A new Chicago Sun Times Article by Neil Steinberg is expands on Chicago's development of the proposed Boomingdale Trail and how it will be unique to Chicago and other similar elevated trails around the world.

Beth White, head of the Chicago office of The Trust for Public Land, said that:

"It's so cool; this is twice as long as the High Line -- three miles -- and connects Logan Square, Wicker Park and Bucktown," said White. "Where it used to be something that divided these communities, now the process of creating this is bringing them together."
