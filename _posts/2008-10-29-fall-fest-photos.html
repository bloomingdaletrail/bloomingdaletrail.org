The kick-off of the new community garden / proto-park at Albany and Whipple at Bloomingdale last Sunday was a great success: TPL, FBT, the City of Chicago, and members of the community were there to celebrate the new sod, grass sofa, and bulb planting with some tasty tamales and hot cocoa.
