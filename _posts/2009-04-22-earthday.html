Here's another fun way to celebrate Earth Day today, Wednesday April 22nd.

FBT and Weegee's Lounge is partnering with the Ames Middle School staff in organizing a great Earth Day event to get the kids involved in the spirit of the day and clean up our neighborhood. Here's a quick snapshot of the day's events:

Volunteer activities:

  1. Walk with the kids and their teachers from Ames Middle School along a route centered on the Bloomingdale Trail and help with a neighborhood clean up and "cheerlead" to spur the kids on. If you want to volunteer, arrive at 12:30 at Ames Middle School soccer field (1920 N. Hamlin at Armitage) and contact David Rodriguez with Ames to check-in.

  2. Man debris collection sites at strategic positions along the Trail and help encourage the kids. If you want to volunteer, stop be the Albany/Whipple park up materials at 1:30 to pick up some bags and gloves prior to heading to the collection site.

  3. Provide a strong showing from the community at the Albany/Whipple park to welcome the kids, finish the clean up and listen to a presentation about Earth Day and The Bloomingdale Trail. If you can join, try and join us at around 2:00

Thanks everyone and Happy Earth Day! Please forward this e-mail to anyone else you think may be interested in participating.
