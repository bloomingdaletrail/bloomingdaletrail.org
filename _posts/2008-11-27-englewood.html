Ben Helphand, Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail board president, and I will be speaking about the Bloomingdale Trail in Englewood. The event is a public discussion on the "Englewood-Heritage Rail Trail", a proposed reuse of an elevated railroad right of way in that community. If you want to learn more about the Bloomingdale Trail or the Englewood project, join us.

The meeting is on Saturday, December 13, 2008, between 9:30am and noon, at the Kelly Library, 6151 S. Normal Boulevard. If you plan to attend, contact Julie Samuels at Openlands, 312.863.6256, jsamuels@openlands.org or John Paul Jones, Friends of the Parks, 312.857.2757, jonesjp@fotp.org. The closest elevated stop is Halsted on the green line.
