--- permalink: /blog/2006/06/12/bloomingdale-trail-walk-with-fotp-cbf/ title: Bloomingdale Trail Walk with FOTP & CBF author: Paul Smith layout: post published: true ---

Join Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail, the Chicagoland Bicycle Federation and Friends of the Parks for a walking tour of the Bloomingdale Trail on Chicago's Northwest Side followed by a reception at Goose Island Brewpub, 1800 N. Clybourn Ave.

The event is the official kick off to the membership collaboration between the Chicagoland Bicycle Federation and Friends of the Parks.

Time: 10:00 am - 12noon walk, 12:00 pm - 2:00 pm reception
Where: The walk begins at McCormick Tribune YMCA, 1834 N. Lawndale Ave.

This event is free for FBT, Friends of the Parks and for Chicagoland Bicycle Federation members. $10 for non-members. For more information about the event or to register, please email Sarah Dunn at dunns@fotp.org or call (312) 857-2757 x. 14
