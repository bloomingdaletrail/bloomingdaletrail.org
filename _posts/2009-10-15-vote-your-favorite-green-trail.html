Patagonia has partnered with The Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail, in Voice Your Choice (VYC), an awareness-raising campaign created to help people get involved in local environmental issues and show their support for local groups.

This year, Patagonia Chicago, located at 1800 N Clybourn Ave has chosen FBT as one of the local enviro orgs to showcase important work with the future Bloomingdale Trail.

You are invited to be heard and make a difference by casting your vote.

From now till October 24, 2009 vote (in store only/no purchase necessary) to help decide how Patagoina should distribute $5000 dollars in grant money to three local environmental organizations.

The top vote-getter will be awarded $2500, with the second- and third- place organizations receiving $1500 and $1000 respectively.

The Friends of the Bloomingdale Trail Board Member Julia, will be there this Saturday, October 17th from 10 AM  to 3 PM to share the latest news with the trail. FBT needs your support so stop in and cast your vote and share your favorite BT story!
